About Us

Welcome to our lab at the Department of physical and optoelectroni Engineering at Shenzhen University.

Our lab is dedicated to developing optofluidic techniques, such as novel optical thermal tweezers, surface plasmon resonance imaging biosensing, Lab-on-chip devices.

To learn more, please visit Members.
